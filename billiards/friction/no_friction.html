No Friction

So, we have now tried playing billiards on ice and in mud. Weird.

But the tiny billiard balls that make up everything are even weirder than that.

They have no friction.

What does that mean?

Give the ball a kick.

Wait for it to stop.

It never stops!

Without friction slowing things down, they never stop.

They keep going forever.
