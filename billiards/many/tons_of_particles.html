Tons of Particles

Now we’re talking!

Well, no.

This is over 1000 particles, and the simulation can’t really handle it. Also, the particles become so small on the screen that you can barely see them, let alone interact with them.

And we’re still so, so far away from the amount of particles in just a single breath.

You would need approximately 10,000,000,000,000,000,000 (or 1019) boxes with 1000 particles in each, just to get the amount of particles in a single breath.
