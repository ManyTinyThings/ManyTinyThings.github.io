Tons of Particles

Okay, let’s go with an amount that the simulation can handle.

Note how, when you smash the particles together, they keep bumping into each other. Every kick you give them results in a chain reaction.

And every time you kick them, they spread out more. At first they’re neatly organised in a tidy triangle. But every time you kick ‘em they get less tidy and more messy.
